QIIYA

As I can't honestly claim I have any "Frequently Asked Questions", I give you "Questions I Imagine You Asking" instead.

And, as the page is, for now, in reconstruction, "Questions" became "For Now, One Question".

FVR? Fervero? Does it mean anything?
It's short for Ferocious Veldt Roarer. It's inspired by George R. R. Martin, of the A Song of Ice And Fire fame. I needed a nickname for the Westeros.org discussion board, and to steal one from a book by GRRM seemed fitting. I'm not explaining the joke further, but I do recommend reading his Tuf Voyaging.
